About Me

I am a designer and technologist creating visual relationships between information and people. I am a former network engineer and a graduate of Rutgers University. My past employment skills cover almost all aspects of the technology technology field, and my academic training is in the visual arts.

Aside from my technical know how, I'm also an avid illustrator, animator, and graphic designer. I'm currently the art director of my own site, El Capitain Ink, a freelance art studio started by my brother and myself in 2010. We originally began the studio as a way to get started in the online comic book industry, but the studio took off as a stand alone studio with a great list of clients.
